Layout of the Role Editor

The layout of the Role Editor is divided in 2 main areas:

  1. The toolbar, that contains the commands to
    • Save the changes applied to the current role
    • Add the role to a given solution
    • Export the role definition in excel or markdown
    • Filter the privileges to show to streamline role editing
  2. The tabs panel, containing
    • A tab to show/edit the list of table related privileges
    • A tab to show/edit the list of not-table related privileges (miscellaneous)
    • The role general information (such as name, business unit, ...)
    • A quick, easy to look at, syntesys of the changes applied to the current role privileges

Take a look on the editing capabilities article for a comprehensive guide about the structure of the tabs.
