Role Inspector

The Role Inspector is a tool that allows you to assess where a given role is actually used within the environment. He looks for the role in the following areas:

  • Users having that specific role assigned
  • Teams having that specific role assigned
  • Forms or apps (or any other dependent solution component) that are associated with that role

The role inspector can be launched from the Role browser, by right-clicking on a selected role and clicking on Inspect usage...

A new window like the followin one will pop up. You can click on the Start inspection button to launch the inspection.

Inspection results are shown in a textual form, that can be freely copied and pasted everywhere.
